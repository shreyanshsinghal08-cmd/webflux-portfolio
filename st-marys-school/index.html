About Our School

A Legacy of Learning & Character

For over 40 years, St. Mary's High School has been a beacon of educational excellence, shaping future leaders through rigorous academics, character development, and a commitment to values that last a lifetime.

🎯

Our Vision

To be a world-class institution nurturing enlightened, ethical citizens.

🚀

Our Mission

Empowering students with knowledge, skills, and values for lifelong success.

💎

Our Values

Integrity, excellence, respect, compassion, and lifelong learning.

Admission Process

1

Online Application

Fill out the online application form with student details and documents.
